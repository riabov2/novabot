The Others Settings section provides extra automation options to enhance your experience with NovaBot. One of its key features is the ability to send automated announcements in the WORLD chat — useful for advertising shops, selling items, or sending alerts to other players.

⚠️ WARNING: Using automated messages in global chat may lead to an account ban! Use this feature responsibly and at your own risk.
