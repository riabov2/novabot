Grab Store

Install Quick Setup Bot Settings Map Settings Elf/Minion Activities Others Coordinates Grab Store

The Grab Store feature allows you to open your personal Store in Divine Realm directly through NovaBot. You can easily configure which items to sell, set their prices, and let NovaBot handle the process automatically.

This automation ensures your shop stays active even while you’re away — helping you earn and manage inventory efficiently without repeated manual setup.
